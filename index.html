How to Delete Your Account

We are sorry to see you go. If you wish to permanently delete your account, please follow the steps outlined below:

  1. Open the app and log into your account.
  2. Navigate to the **Settings** section of the app by tapping the gear icon in the top-right corner of the screen.
  3. Scroll down to find the **Delete Account** option.
  4. Tap **Delete Account**. You will be prompted with a confirmation message.
  5. Confirm your decision by tapping **Confirm Deletion**. Please note that this action is irreversible, and all your data will be permanently removed.

If you experience any issues or have any questions, please do not hesitate to contact our support team at support@ajrli.com.
