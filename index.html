Account Deletion Instructions

If you wish to delete your account, please follow the steps below:

  1. Open the app and log into your account.
  2. Navigate to the **Settings** section in the app.
  3. Scroll down to the bottom and click on the **Delete Account** option.
  4. You will be asked to confirm your decision. Click **Confirm** to permanently delete your account.

If you encounter any issues, please contact our support team at support@example.com.
